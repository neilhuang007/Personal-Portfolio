About Me

Hi, I'm Neil—a high school student who's been captivated by code since I first started programming. What began as curiosity has evolved into a passion that consumes most of my free time

Currently, I'm diving deep into C, Rust, and Go—languages that let me build lightning-fast backends and work closer to the metal. There's something incredibly satisfying about optimizing performance and creating efficient, native-level applications.

Engineering became my superpower because it transformed my imaginations into tangible reality. As a kid, I was mesmerized watching hackers in movies navigate through glowing terminals and build incredible applications. Now I get to live that dream. My current interests span reverse engineering, full-stack web development, prompt engineering, and building AI-powered startups.

What I find fascinating about engineering is its emotional rollercoaster. Every project starts with boundless motivation and ambitious vision. Then comes the grind—debugging, refactoring, and problem-solving that can be genuinely frustrating. But the moment everything clicks and the project comes to life? That rush of achievement is addictive and immediately fuels my next ambitious build.
